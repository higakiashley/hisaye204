Artist Statement

My name is Ashley Higaki and I am a mixed media artist. I am currently pursuing Studio Art at San Jose State University. My artwork is a representation of myself and my appreciation for my culture. My main focus is Traditional Japanese Art concentrating on unique patterns and utilizing pens, sumi ink and charcoal for my pieces. Another medium that I like to work with is glass. I have done a few glass etching projects that are focused on botanical subjects. I enjoy working with multiple mediums to showcase my creativity and to also incorporate traditional mediums to make my pieces even more special.

I have shown work in a group exhibition in Gallery 5 at San Jose State. I have a strong passion for representing my culture in both traditional and experimental ways. I am hoping to share my culture through my work and show who I am as well. I am always striving to create meaningful pieces and pushing boundaries within myself.
